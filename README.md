# 🛒 Green Mart
Your Fresh Grocery Shopping App

# 📱 About
Green Mart is a modern grocery shopping app built with Flutter. It provides a smooth and elegant experience for browsing fresh products, managing your cart, and handling your profile — all in one place.

#✨ Features
FeatureDescription
- 🔐 AuthSign up & Login with form validation
- 🏪 ProductsBrowse fresh products with clean UI
- 🗂️ CategoriesExplore products by category
- 🛒 CartAdd, remove & manage your shopping cart
- 👤 ProfileView and manage your account
